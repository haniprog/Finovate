Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new asset to my portfolio?

Go to Portfolio → Click "Add Asset" → Fill in the details and save.

How is my net worth calculated?

Net worth = Total Assets − Total Liabilities. This updates automatically.

How do I add or remove transactions?

Open Transactions, use Add transaction to save entries (stored in your browser), and click Remove on a row to delete it.

Can I export my transaction history?

Go to Transactions and use your browser to print or save the table if needed. A dedicated CSV export may be added later.

How do budget alerts work?

When spending reaches about 90% of a category budget in Budgeting, you will see a notification-style reminder in the app.

How do I change language, password visibility, or profile details?

Use Settings for language, currency, 2FA, and editable profile fields (with a show/hide control for the password field). Use My Profile for your photo, bio, and linked bank or e-wallet labels.

How does linking my bank or e-wallet work?

On My Profile, the "Linked accounts" section lets you record which bank or e-wallet you use with Finovate for your own reference. It does not connect to real banking APIs—never share real passwords or full account numbers.

Where does Recent Activity come from?

Recent Activity on your profile shows your latest saved transactions (same data as the Transactions page).
